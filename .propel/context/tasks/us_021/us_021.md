# User Story - US_021

## Story ID
   * US_021

## Story Title
   * Walk-in Patient Registration

## Description
  * As a staff member, I want to quickly register walk-in patients who arrive without appointments, so that they can be added to today's queue and seen by available providers.

## Acceptance Criteria
  1. **Given** a patient walks in without an appointment, **When** I click "Register Walk-in" button on the queue management page (SCR-009) and fill the quick registration form (patient name, phone, date of birth, chief complaint, preferred provider if any), **Then** the system creates a same-day appointment entry with status="Walk-in", appointment_datetime=current timestamp, estimated_wait_time calculated based on current queue length (avg 15 min per patient), adds to queue list with "Walk-in" badge, assigns next available provider if no preference specified, sends SMS to patient "You're checked in. Estimated wait: [X] minutes. We'll call your name.", and logs the walk-in registration to audit log.

## Edge Cases
   * What happens when walk-in patient is already registered in system? (Search by phone/DOB, auto-fill existing patient details, link to existing patient record)
   * How is walk-in prioritized vs scheduled appointments? (Walk-ins added to end of current time slot queue, urgent walk-ins marked with priority flag move to front)
   * What if all providers are fully booked? (Calculate estimated wait time based on last appointment end time + buffer, offer to schedule for next available slot instead)
