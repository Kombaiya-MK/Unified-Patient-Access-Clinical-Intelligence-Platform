# User Story - US_016

## Story ID
   * US_016

## Story Title
   * Automated Appointment Reminders (SMS/Email)

## Description
  * As a patient, I want to receive automated reminders 24 hours before my appointment via SMS and email, so that I don't forget my scheduled visit and the clinic reduces no-show rates.

## Acceptance Criteria
  1. **Given** I have an appointment scheduled for tomorrow, **When** the automated reminder job runs (scheduled cron job every hour checking appointments 24 hours ahead), **Then** the system sends me an SMS message "Reminder: You have an appointment tomorrow at [time] with [provider] at [location]. Reply CONFIRM or call [phone] to cancel." and an email with appointment details, location map link, preparation instructions, and calendar attachment (.ics file), logs the reminder sent status (reminders_sent_at timestamp in Appointments table), and retries up to 3 times if SMS/email delivery fails.

## Edge Cases
   * What happens when patient has multiple appointments within 24 hours? (Send one consolidated message listing all appointments)
   * How is SMS delivery failure handled? (Log failure, attempt email as fallback notification method, display warning in staff dashboard)
   * What if patient opts out of reminders? (Check notifications_preferences table, skip reminder if opt_out=true for reminder category)
