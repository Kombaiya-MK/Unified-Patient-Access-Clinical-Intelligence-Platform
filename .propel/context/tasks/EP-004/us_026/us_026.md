# User Story - US_026

## Story ID
   * US_026

## Story Title
   * Manual Intake Form with AI Switch

## Description
  * As a patient, I want a traditional form-based intake option that I can switch to from AI mode at any time, so that I have flexibility to use whichever method I'm more comfortable with.

## Acceptance Criteria
  1. **Given** I am on the patient intake page (SCR-007), **When** I click "Switch to Manual Form" button (available in both AI and manual modes), **Then** the system transitions to form view displaying all intake fields organized in sections (Demographics, Chief Complaint, Medical History, Current Medications, Allergies, Previous Surgeries, Family History), pre-fills any data already collected via AI conversation from the summary panel without data loss, shows progress indicator at top showing completed sections, allows me to fill fields at my own pace with inline validation, and saves draft automatically every 30 seconds to prevent data loss if browser crashes.

## Edge Cases
   * What happens when switching from manual to AI mid-form? (Preserve all entered form data, AI resumes asking about incomplete sections only)
   * How are required fields enforced? (Show asterisks on required fields, prevent submission if incomplete, scroll to first missing field on submit attempt)
   * What if patient has JavaScript disabled? (Fallback to server-side rendered form with POST submission, show "Enable JavaScript for better experience" banner)
