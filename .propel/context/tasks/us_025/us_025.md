# User Story - US_025

## Story ID
   * US_025

## Story Title
   * AI Conversational Intake Interface

## Description
  * As a patient, I want to complete my intake information through an AI-powered conversation that feels natural and validates my responses in real-time, so that I can provide accurate medical information quickly without filling tedious forms.

## Acceptance Criteria
  1. **Given** I am a patient with a scheduled appointment, **When** I navigate to the intake page (SCR-007), **Then** I see a chat interface with AI assistant greeting me by name and asking for chief complaint/reason for visit as the first question.

  2. **Given** I am in an AI conversation, **When** I type a response and submit, **Then** the AI validates the response format, asks relevant follow-up questions based on medical context, and displays my responses in a readable summary panel on the right side with <3s response latency.

  3. **Given** I am partway through AI intake, **When** I click "Switch to Manual Form", **Then** the system transitions seamlessly to traditional form (SCR-007 manual mode) with all AI-collected data pre-filled in corresponding fields without data loss.

  4. **Given** AI intake is complete, **When** I click "Submit Intake", **Then** the system validates all required fields (>98% accuracy per AIR-R03), saves to ClinicalDocuments table, displays confirmation "Intake submitted successfully", and notifies assigned staff member.

## Edge Cases
   * What happens when AI response is ambiguous or unclear? (AI asks clarifying question: "I want to make sure I understand - you meant [interpretation]?")
   * How does the system handle offensive or inappropriate patient inputs? (AI responds professionally: "I'm here to help with medical information. Let's focus on your health concerns.", logs incident)
   * What if OpenAI API fails mid-conversation? (Circuit breaker triggers, system displays "AI temporarily unavailable" with automatic switch to manual form, preserves conversation history)
   * How is personally identifiable information (PII) handled? (PII redaction layer before sending to OpenAI per NFR-003 HIPAA compliance)
