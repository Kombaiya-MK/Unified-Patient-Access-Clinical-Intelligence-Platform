# User Story - US_015

## Story ID
   * US_015

## Story Title
   * Waitlist Management and Dynamic Slot Swapping

## Description
  * As a patient, I want to join a waitlist for fully-booked time slots and receive automatic notifications when my preferred slot becomes available, so that I can get my desired appointment time without constantly checking.

## Acceptance Criteria
  1. **Given** my preferred time slot is fully booked, **When** I click "Join Waitlist" and confirm, **Then** the system adds me to the wail list (Waitlist table with appointment_id, patient_id, preferred_datetime, priority_score, created_at), sends confirmation "You're on the waitlist for [date/time]. We'll notify you if it becomes available", and when another patient cancels that slot, automatically sends me notification (email + dashboard popup) within 5 minutes and holds the slot for 2 hours pending my acceptance.

## Edge Cases
   * What happens when multiple patients are on waitlist for same slot? (First-come-first-served based on created_at timestamp, notify next person if first declines)
   * How long does waitlist entry remain active? (Expire after appointment date passes, send reminder 24 hours before asking to confirm interest)
   * What if patient doesn't respond to slot availability notification within 2 hours? (Auto-release slot, notify next person on waitlist)
