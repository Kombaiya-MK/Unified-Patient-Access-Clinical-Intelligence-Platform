# User Story - US_022

## Story ID
   * US_022

## Story Title
   * Arrival Marking and Status Update Functions

## Description
  * As a staff member, I want to mark when patients arrive and update their status through their visit journey, so that the clinic tracks patient flow and wait times accurately.

## Acceptance Criteria
  1. **Given** a patient on the queue with status="Scheduled", **When** I click "Mark Arrived" button next to their name, **Then** the system updates status to "Arrived", records arrival_time timestamp, moves the appointment to "Arrived" section of the queue, triggers real-time WebSocket update to refresh all staff viewing the queue within 5 seconds, logs the status change to audit log, and displays success toast "Patient marked as arrived".

## Edge Cases
   * What happens when staff tries to mark arrived more than once? (Display message "Already marked as arrived at [timestamp]", prevent duplicate updates)
   * How is late arrival tracked? (If arrival_time > appointment_datetime + 15min, flag as "Late" with orange indicator)
   * What if patient leaves before being seen? (Add "Mark Left Without Being Seen" button, status becomes "No Show")
