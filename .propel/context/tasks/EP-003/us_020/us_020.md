# User Story - US_020

## Story ID
   * US_020

## Story Title
   * Staff Queue Management Interface

## Description
  * As a staff member, I want a real-time queue management interface showing today's appointments with status tracking, so that I can efficiently manage patient flow and update appointment statuses throughout the day.

## Acceptance Criteria
  1. **Given** I am logged in as staff, **When** I access the queue management page (SCR-009), **Then** I see today's appointment list with columns: Patient Name, Appointment Time, Status (Scheduled/Arrived/In Progress/Completed/No Show), Provider, Department, and Action buttons.

  2. **Given** a patient arrives for their appointment, **When** I click "Mark Arrived" next to their appointment, **Then** the system updates the status to "Arrived", logs the arrival time in audit log, moves the appointment to "Arrived" section, and triggers real-time update to other staff viewing the same queue (<5s latency).

  3. **Given** an appointment is in "Arrived" status, **When** I click "Start Consultation", **Then** the system updates status to "In Progress", records start time, and displays appointment duration timer.

  4. **Given** a consultation is complete, **When** I click "Mark Completed", **Then** the system updates status to "Completed", calculates total duration, archives the appointment from active queue, and triggers billing workflow notification.

## Edge Cases
   * What happens when a patient scheduled for 2:00 PM doesn't arrive by 2:30 PM? (Orange alert badge appears, staff can click "Mark No Show")
   * How does the system handle walk-in patients not in scheduled appointments? (US-021 covers walk-in registration, creates same-day appointment entry)
   * What if two staff members try to mark arrived simultaneously? (Optimistic locking: first update wins, second receives "Already marked arrived by [Staff Name]" message)
   * How is the queue filtered for high-volume days? (Filter dropdowns: Status, Provider, Department; Search by patient name)

> **Wireframe Details (SCR-009):**
> - **Queue table**: Sortable by time/status, row highlight on hover, color-coded status badges (green=Arrived, blue=In Progress, gray=Scheduled)
> - **Action buttons**: "Mark Arrived", "Start", "Complete", "No Show" - contextual based on current status
> - **Real-time updates**: WebSocket connection displays notification banner when queue changes ("New patient arrived")
> - **Filters panel**: Status filter (multi-select), Provider dropdown, Department dropdown, Date picker (default: today)
> - **No-show risk indicator**: Orange badge with "⚠ High Risk" next to patient name if no-show risk score ≥70
> - **Walk-in registration button**: Top-right corner, opens modal for quick registration
> - **Responsive design**: Mobile (cards), tablet/desktop (table layout)
