# User Story - US_023

## Story ID
   * US_023

## Story Title
   * Staff Appointment Booking on Behalf of Patients

## Description
  * As a staff member, I want to book appointments for patients who call or visit the front desk, so that I can assist patients who prefer phone/in-person booking over online self-service.

## Acceptance Criteria
  1. **Given** a patient requests appointment booking via phone or in-person, **When** I search for the patient by phone/email/name, select them from results, choose appointment date/time/provider on the booking interface (same as patient self-booking but with staff override options), and click "Book Appointment", **Then** the system creates the appointment with booked_by_staff=true and booked_by_staff_id=[my user ID], sends confirmation email/SMS to patient, generates PDF confirmation, logs the staff-assisted booking to audit log showing who booked on behalf of whom, and displays success "Appointment booked for [patient name] on [date/time]".

## Acceptance Criteria
   * What happens when patient doesn't exist in system? (Display "Patient not found" with "Register New Patient" button, opens registration form)
   * How are staff override options different from patient booking? (Staff can book past same-day cutoff time, override full slots with "Override Capacity" checkbox if urgent)
   * What if staff's own appointment conflicts? (System allows booking regardless, staff manages their own schedule separately)
