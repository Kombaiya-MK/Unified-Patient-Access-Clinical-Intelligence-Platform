# User Story - US_024

## Story ID
   * US_024

## Story Title
   * Mark No-Show Functionality

## Description
  * As a staff member, I want to mark appointments as no-show when patients don't arrive, so that the system tracks no-show history for risk assessment and billing purposes.

## Acceptance Criteria
  1. **Given** an appointment with status="Scheduled" and appointment_datetime has passed by >30 minutes, **When** I click "Mark No-Show" button next to the patient's name and optionally add a note in the confirmation dialog, **Then** the system updates status to "No Show", records no_show_marked_at timestamp and marked_by_staff_id, increments the patient's no_show_count in their profile, logs the action to audit log with note if provided, removes appointment from active queue, triggers no-show risk score recalculation for patient's future bookings (increases risk score by 30 points), and displays confirmation "Appointment marked as No Show".

## Edge Cases
   * What happens when appointment is marked no-show but patient arrives late? (Allow staff to revert: "Undo No-Show" button available for 2 hours, changes status back to "Arrived")
   * How are excused no-shows handled? (Add "Excused No-Show" checkbox in dialog, doesn't increment no_show_count or affect risk score)
   * What if patient cancels online while staff marking no-show? (System detects conflict, displays "Patient cancelled this appointment", prevents duplicate updates)
